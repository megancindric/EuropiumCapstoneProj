Walkabout

Walking doesn't have to be boring! With this innovative technology designed by Sharon Philipps, you can create fun and engaging walks right in your own neighborhood!

Get started today

How it Works:

Using Google Maps API & Google Directions, we're able to create dynamic, custom routes in your community! We'll keep track of your distance, time, and more importantly, your points!

Points? Tell me more!

It's not the destination, it's the journey! We'll help you track every statue, tree, and animal friend you pass while you're out on your walkabout. Each marker you place will add to your total score, and when your walk is finished you'll have the chance to add pictures, save your routes, and more!
